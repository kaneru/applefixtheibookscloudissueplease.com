Apple, fix the iBooks cloud issue, please!

I'm done! This is nonsense!

iBooks has this annoying "feature" that is that your books are constantly moved to iCloud for no good reason. Even if there is plenty of free space on disk.

What if I'm on a plane, Apple? I would like to read my books that I had downloaded the day or two before, but they are gone from my device!

I don't want to turn off iBooks from using iCloud. I really like the feature that all my bookmarks and notes from books are synced across my devices in iBooks. It is really convienient. What is not convienient is that I can't read my books like that. Why do I have to download them from the cloud again and again?

It should not be like that. I refuse to believe that this is the way iBooks and my books work together.

Apple, prevent iBooks from moving books to the cloud, please!
